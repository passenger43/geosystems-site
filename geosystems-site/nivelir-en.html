Nivelir

A field helper for builders, site managers and surveyors.

  • Calculate instrument horizon from a benchmark and staff reading.
  • Setting-out elevations and required staff readings.
  • Level survey and an elevation library.

Features

For elevation work

Benchmark

Set the reference benchmark and the staff reading.

Instrument horizon

Calculate the height of collimation and working elevations.

Setting out

Get the required staff reading for a design elevation.

Level survey

Enter staff readings and calculate actual elevations.

Library

Store benchmarks, working points and elevations.

Export

Transfer results for further work and reports.
