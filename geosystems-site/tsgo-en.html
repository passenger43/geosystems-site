TS Go!

A field tool for surveyors: total station, project, DXF, stakeout and control.

  • Bluetooth connection between smartphone and total station.
  • Work with projects and DXF layout files.
  • Check measured points against design lines, points, objects or surfaces.

Features

What TS Go! does

The app is built around a simple field workflow: open a project, select an object, measure a point and immediately understand the result.

Total station

Connect to supported instruments and receive measured point coordinates.

DXF project

Open layout files, display CAD objects and work with lines, points, arcs and circles.

Stakeout

Field parameters against the project: chainage, offset, up/down, left/right, forward/back and turn angle.

Base lines

Create working base lines from points and reverse line direction quickly.

Survey

A lightweight workspace for measured points, saving, export and cleanup.

Surfaces

Elevation control against design 3D surfaces is being developed in current versions.
