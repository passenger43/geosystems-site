Privacy policy

How information may be processed when using the GeoSystems studio website and mobile applications.

1. General provisions

This policy explains what information may be processed by sole proprietor Ilya Maltsev, Tax ID 434592932969, Registration No. 311434501800091 (the “Operator”), when users interact with geosystems-studio.ru and GeoSystems studio mobile applications.

2. Information that may be processed

  • contact information voluntarily provided when contacting support;
  • a technical device identifier used for trial and PRO access verification;
  • application identifier, access status, access expiration time and server verification time;
  • technical order and payment result data needed to provide access;
  • other information voluntarily included by the user in a support request.

3. Payment card data

The Operator does not receive or store full bank card details. Payment details are entered on the payment service side and processed under that service's own documents.

4. Processing purposes

  • providing a trial period and paid PRO access;
  • verifying application access status;
  • handling user requests and support cases;
  • preventing erroneous or duplicate access activation;
  • meeting applicable legal requirements.

5. Processing basis

Information is processed in connection with website use, user requests, access ordering and the Operator's performance of obligations to the User.

6. Sharing with third parties

Information may be shared only to the extent reasonably necessary for payment infrastructure, compliance with legal requirements or protection of lawful interests of the Operator and the User.

When paying, the user interacts with the Robokassa payment page. Data processing on the Robokassa side is governed by its own legal documents.

7. Storage and security

The Operator applies reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect processed information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

8. User requests

Questions related to privacy, clarification of data or related requests may be sent to geosystemsstudio@yandex.ru.

9. Policy updates

The Operator may update this policy. The current version is published on this page.
